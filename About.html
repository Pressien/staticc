About This Website

Welcome to the Nth Room Case website. This interactive journey takes you through a detective's investigation of a digital crime syndicate, focusing on the dangers posed by deepfake technology and its connection to criminal activities like trafficking. As you navigate through different scenarios, you'll make decisions that affect the outcome of the investigation. The purpose of this website is to raise awareness of the risks of deepfake technology and the importance of using digital tools responsibly to combat crime and protect society.
