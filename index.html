Deepfake is a machine technology used to manipulate images, audios, and even videos. In today’s world, it serves as entertainment for some, but it can objectify and exploit innocent individuals. The threat it carries is substantial: "What will society think? Will they believe it’s Deepfake?" The technology blurs the line between truth and manipulation, leaving the victims with fear, anxiety, and social repercussions.

We need a brave detective for this case, Can you save the victims?
